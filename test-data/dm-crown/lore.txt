Campaign Setting: The Shattered Realms

The world was once whole, but a great cataclysm 1000 years ago split reality into floating islands connected by magical portals. Each realm developed its own culture.

Major Realms:
- Skyreach: Cloud cities of scholars and mages
- Ironhold: Underground dwarven kingdoms
- Wildwood: Endless forests of the fey
- Ashfall: Volcanic wastelands ruled by dragons

The players seek the Fragments of Unity, ancient artifacts that could restore the world.
